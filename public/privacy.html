Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2024

Introduction

Behavior School LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates Kiwi Voice and is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Kiwi Voice is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No accounts or sign-ups required
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Custom communication boards and icons
  • Voice recordings (if you record custom audio)
  • Usage progress and settings
  • Learner profile information

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the backup feature.

Microphone Access

Kiwi Voice may request microphone access to allow you to record custom voice prompts. Audio recordings are stored locally and are never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party integrations:

  • OpenMoji: Symbol images are fetched from OpenMoji's open-source library (CC BY-SA 4.0)
  • Superwall: For in-app purchase management (no personal data shared)

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Kiwi Voice is designed for use by children under parental/guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. All app data remains on the device and under the control of the parent/guardian.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Using device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • Regularly backing up your data using the in-app export feature
  • Using the Lock Mode feature when the app is in use by a child

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Download your data using the Backup feature
  • Delete: Use the Reset function to delete all app data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
